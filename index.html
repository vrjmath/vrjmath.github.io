Viraj Shitole

I am a Master’s student in the Electrical and Computer Engineering Department at Georgia Tech, advised by Professor Pan Li. My current research focuses on machine learning, foundation models, and generative graph algorithms.

Prior to Georgia Tech, I received Bachelor’s degrees in Computer Engineering, Mathematics, and Statistics from the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. At UIUC, I was advised by Professor Elyse Rosenbaum and worked on building machine learning models for transistors.
